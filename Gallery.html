Review

Ipoh is a haven for nature lovers and cultural explorers because it is surrounded by breathtaking limestone mountains and caves. Ipoh is known as the City of Hills because of the unusual geological formations, temples, and caverns found in these picturesque mountains.

Known for its limestone hills, cave temples, colonial architecture, colorful street art, and renowned culinary scene, Ipoh is one of Malaysia's most underappreciated cities.
